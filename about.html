Our Mission

To inspire individuality, expression and creativity and show that art shouldn't just be confined to gallery walls.

A place where people can come to be inspired, whether they're learning about different art forms and diverse practises, or reading the stories and tales behind each artwork. More than that, our amazing community can take art home with them, experiencing art in their everyday. We soften the edges of modernity with the best sides of romanticism.

Our Values
01 Self-Expression

We want to share creative inspiration, imagination, promote self-expression and thinking outside the box.

02 Supporting One Another

From the Panda community to our designer collaborations, we are constantly listening and focused on making changes to support them.

03 Individuality

You can be part of the process, to get creative, to design something as unique as you for your phone needs.

Commited to a Better Future

As part of our constant evolution and pursuit of quality, we remain constantly focused on any impacts our actions might have both environmentally and socially.

The Panda and our supply chain partners are committed to meeting and constantly improving on the highest standards of product development, manufacturing and delivery. Our phone cases are made to order to help reduce any unnecessary waste by creating each order only when you purchase. With production in Australia, the United Kingdom, the United States and Italy we are not only get your art for your everyday to you faster, but we also reduce our carbon footprint. We're constantly looking to improve these processes and our materials as technologies advance.
