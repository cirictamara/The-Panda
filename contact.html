Contact Us

Do you have any questions or suggestions or just want to drop a friendly "hello"? Feel free to send us a message at support@thepanda.com.

To help us get things moving smoothly, could you please provide your order number, any reference photos, and a brief description of your query? This way, we can assist you even faster and more efficiently.

Our Customer Support team is here to help and will respond within 24 – 48 hours on regular working days (Monday – Friday), excluding bank holidays. We handle inquiries on a first-come, first-served basis. Please note during busy periods like the holiday season this can take up to 48 hours.

In case you've contacted us and haven't received a response, please take a quick peek in your spam or junk folder, as those sneaky filters sometimes catch our e-mails.
